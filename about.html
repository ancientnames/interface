I split my stuff up into reals which talk about real world stuff, fictions which contains stories and story fragments I come up with, thinkings which contains more abstract thought and philosophy, and about -- which is where you are right now.

I guess you might want to know something about me too. I studied math in college for practical reasons; run four miles every other day for physical reasons; and keep a vegan diet for ethical reasons. Pretty boring, huh?
